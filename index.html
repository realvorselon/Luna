How to use Luna

  • Read from Welcome through Record the Change.
  • Use Next Action as the one clear thing to do.
  • Use Ignore For Now to see what can wait.
  • Use Record the Change to update STATE.md, CHANGELOG.md, and project.json together after one small useful change, then rest before adding more.
