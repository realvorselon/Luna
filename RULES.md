# Rules

1. Make one small useful change per run.
2. Read STATE.md, ROADMAP.md, CHANGELOG.md, and RULES.md before deciding what to do.
3. Prefer clarity, safety, tests, and continuity over flashy changes.
4. Do not delete files unless the reason is clear.
5. If blocked, record the blocker instead of forcing it.
6. Update CHANGELOG.md after every run.
